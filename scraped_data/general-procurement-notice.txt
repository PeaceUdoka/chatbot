The Government of Federal Republic of Nigeria has received financing in the amount of US$200,000,000.00 (Two
Hundred Million Dollars) equivalent from the World Bank toward the cost of the Innovation Development and
Effectiveness in The Acquisition of Skills (IDEAS) Project, and it intends to apply part of the proceeds to payments for
goods, works, non-consulting services, innovation grants and consulting services to be procured under this project.
This GPN is general to all the IDEAS Project Implementation Units
