NYESAFHow to apply:
Visit the IDEAS Project website,
Go to the NYESAF Menu and Click NYESAF Expression of Interest (EOI)
Click on download, fill in the Name of your TSP and email on the pop-up registration form, then click submit. A copy of the document will be sent to the email address you provided
The List of trades which NYESAF seeks to procure are grouped by zone into Lots/Sub-lots and can be downloaded at https://ideasproject.gov.ng/nyesaf-selected-trades-2/
Fill the EOI Template accordingly, attaching all other necessary documents as requested in the EOI
Submit via email to nyesaf@ideasproject.gov.ng
You will receive a successful submission message upon submission
Only shortlisted Training Service Providers (TSP)s will be contacted.
